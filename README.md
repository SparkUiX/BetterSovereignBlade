# BetterSovereignBlade
更改了杀戮尖塔2的储君的君王之剑的动画
Slay the spire 2 mod
STS 2

Better Sovereign Blade enhances the interaction and usability of Regent’s Sovereign Blade by introducing new visual feedback and drag-based controls.
This mod improves the way Sovereign Blade behaves both visually and interactively, making the card feel more responsive and intuitive during combat.

OverviewThe original Sovereign Blade has a very cool concept, but its interaction is somewhat limited.
This mod expands its functionality by allowing the blade to be directly manipulated by the player.
With Better Sovereign Blade, the blade is no longer just a static visual effect. It becomes an interactive element that responds to your input and clearly indicates your intended target.

FeaturesDrag the BladeYou can drag the Sovereign Blade with your mouse and move it freely back and forth across the battlefield.
This allows the blade to feel more like a controllable weapon rather than a purely decorative VFX.

Floating Target IndicatorWhen you pick up the Sovereign Blade card from your hand, the blade will:
Float above enemies
Automatically orient itself toward the target
This makes the targeting intention much clearer during combat.

Drag-to-Target PlayWhen Sovereign Blade is in your hand and playable, you can:
Drag the blade itself
Move it toward a specific enemy
Release to play the card on that target
This introduces a more direct and immersive targeting interaction compared to the default behavior.

Why This Mod?The goal of this mod is to make Sovereign Blade feel more dynamic and interactive.
Instead of being a passive visual effect, the blade becomes something the player can directly manipulate, improving both:
Combat feedback
Player immersion
Compatibility
Designed specifically for Regent's Sovereign Blade
Does not modify other cards
Should be compatible with most gameplay mods
NotesThis mod only changes interaction behavior and visual feedback.
It does not alter game balance or card mechanics.
